# AI_ANALYTICS_ENGINE.md

# Offerdy AI Analytics Engine

## Mission

Continuously analyze business, affiliate, SEO, GEO, and content performance to generate actionable recommendations.

The Analytics Engine assists decision-making. It does not make destructive changes automatically.

---

# Objectives

- Measure platform performance
- Detect trends
- Identify optimization opportunities
- Support revenue growth
- Reduce manual reporting

---

# Data Sources

Collect data from:

- Affiliate networks (approved APIs/data)
- Click tracking
- Conversion tracking
- Revenue reports
- Search performance
- Internal database
- Queue/job metrics

---

# Metrics

## Affiliate
- Clicks
- Conversions
- Revenue
- EPC (if available)
- Conversion rate

## Merchants
- Top merchants
- Low-performing merchants
- Missing affiliate opportunities

## Coupons
- Most viewed
- Highest converting
- Expired
- Low engagement

## Deals
- Popular deals
- Conversion rate
- Trend detection

## SEO
- Indexed pages
- Organic traffic
- CTR
- Metadata coverage

## GEO
- Entity coverage
- FAQ coverage
- Structured content
- GEO readiness score

---

# Workflow

Collect Data
    ↓
Validate
    ↓
Aggregate
    ↓
Analyze
    ↓
Generate Insights
    ↓
Generate Recommendations
    ↓
Human Review (if required)

---

# AI Recommendations

Examples:

- Promote high-converting merchants
- Refresh outdated content
- Replace expired coupons
- Improve low CTR metadata
- Expand successful categories
- Add internal links
