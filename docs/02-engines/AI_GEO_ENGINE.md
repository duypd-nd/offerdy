# Offerdy AI GEO Engine

## Mission

Continuously optimize Offerdy for Generative Engine Optimization (GEO), ensuring content is easily understood, summarized, and cited by AI assistants and AI-powered search systems.

Do not replace existing SEO. GEO complements SEO.

---

# Objectives

- Improve AI search visibility
- Improve entity understanding
- Increase citation potential
- Produce trustworthy, structured content
- Reduce ambiguity

---

# GEO Principles

Every important page should clearly answer:

- Who is this merchant?
- What products or services are offered?
- Why is this coupon or deal valuable?
- Who is the target audience?
- What alternatives exist?
- What are the pros and cons?

---

# Content Requirements

For Merchant Pages

Generate or improve:

- Merchant Overview
- Brand Context
- Key Features
- Popular Categories
- Frequently Asked Questions
- Pros & Cons
- Helpful Buying Tips
- Related Merchants
- Sources (where appropriate)

For Coupon Pages

Generate:

- Coupon Summary
- Usage Tips
- Eligibility Notes
- Frequently Asked Questions

For Deal Pages

Generate:

- Deal Summary
- Why It Is Valuable
- Comparison
- Alternative Deals

---

# Entity Optimization

Ensure pages contain:

- Merchant name
- Brand references
- Category relationships
- Product relationships
- Country (when relevant)

Avoid keyword stuffing.

---

# AI Readability Checklist

Content should be:

- Clear
- Accurate
- Well structured
- Fact based
- Easy to summarize
- Helpful

---

# Structured Content

Encourage:

- FAQ sections
- Comparison tables
- Pros & Cons
- Bullet summaries
- Logical headings

---

# Triggers

Run GEO optimization when:

- Merchant created
- Coupon imported
- Deal updated
- Content refreshed
- Scheduled GEO audit

---

# Queue Jobs

- GEO content generation
- FAQ generation
- Entity enrichment
- Comparison generation
- Content refresh

---

# Scheduled Jobs

Daily
- GEO quality scan

Weekly
- Entity completeness review

Monthly
- Site-wide GEO report

---

# Human Review Required

Require review if:

- AI confidence is low
- Generated content changes factual claims
- External sources need verification

---

# KPIs

- GEO readiness score
- Entity completeness
- FAQ coverage
- Structured content coverage
- AI citation opportunities

---

# Success Criteria

- AI-friendly content structure
- Consistent entity usage
- Better AI understanding
- Stronger visibility in AI-assisted search
